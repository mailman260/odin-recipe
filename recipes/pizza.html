Pizza

Description

An italian classic made for Americans

Have you ever wanted to combine dough, cheese, tomato sauce, and toppings? This is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Topping of your choice

Steps

  1. Spread the dough out into a 12" circle
  2. Lay a thin layer of sauce on top of the dough
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top of the sauced area
  4. Add desired toppings
  5. Bake at 450 degrees farenheit for 20 mintues
