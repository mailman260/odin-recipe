Alfredo

Description

Who said dinner has to be hard? Take this four step pasta for a spin

You will love this recipe; I guarentee it

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Spaghetti
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Chicken (optional)
  • Salt 'n Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta according to package instructions
  2. While pasta is cooking heat the sauce in a saucepan
  3. If the sauce finishes first keep the heat on simmer
  4. When both are done simply combine and enjoy!
